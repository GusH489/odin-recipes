How to make Yakiniku

Description

Yakiniku is a japanese dish that means "grilled meat" and is a vibrant and interactive dining experience that brings people together around the grill. Thin slices of marinated or plain meat are grilled at the table along with a colorful array of vegetables. Each bit is dipped in a rich, savory-sweet sauce (tare) made with soy sauce, mirin, garlic, and sesame oil, enhancing the umami flavors of the grilled ingredients.
